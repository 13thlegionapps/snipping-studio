Privacy Policy

Snipping Studio  ·  13th Legion, LLC  ·  Last updated: May 2026

Snipping Studio is a macOS screen capture and annotation tool published by 13th Legion, LLC. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and what we do with it — which is nothing, because the app operates entirely on your device.

No Data Collection

Snipping Studio does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app contains no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and no telemetry of any kind. It makes no network requests.

Screen Recording

Snipping Studio requests Screen Recording permission through macOS Privacy & Security settings solely to capture your screen when you initiate a capture. No screen content is ever transmitted off your device. Screenshots are stored locally in the app's sandboxed container on your Mac.

Capture History

Your last 10 screenshots are stored locally in the app's private Application Support folder. This data never leaves your Mac. You can clear your history at any time from within the app, or by uninstalling it.

Preferences

Your settings (keyboard shortcut, default export format, auto-copy preference) are stored in macOS User Defaults on your device. This data is never shared.

Third Parties

Snipping Studio does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks outside of Apple's own operating system frameworks (SwiftUI, ScreenCaptureKit, AppKit, Vision).

Children's Privacy

Snipping Studio does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
